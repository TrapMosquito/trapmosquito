The Science

How It Works

Mosquitoes are attracted to the CO₂ we exhale. Our trap mimics that signal with a precision release of pure carbon dioxide, drawing mosquitoes in from up to an acre away and trapping them permanently.

01

CO₂ Lure

The machine emits a continuous, calibrated stream of carbon dioxide that perfectly mimics human breath, attracting mosquitoes from a wide radius without alerting them to any danger.

02

Capture & Contain

A silent fan draws mosquitoes into a fine mesh collection bag the moment they approach the source. Once inside, dehydration eliminates them naturally, with zero chemicals involved.

03

Monthly Service

Our team visits monthly to swap the CO₂ tank, remove the collection bag, and ensure everything is running at peak performance. You do nothing.
